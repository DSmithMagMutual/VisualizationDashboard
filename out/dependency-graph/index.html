How to Use

• Epics are shown as larger circles, Stories as smaller circles

• Node colors represent status: Red (To Do), Orange (In Progress), Green (Done)

• Border colors represent teams

• Lines show dependencies between epics and their stories

• Drag nodes to rearrange the graph layout

• Hover over nodes to see detailed information
